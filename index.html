Crafto
Product Case Study

Crafto is a platform designed to empower independent creators and small artisan businesses to discover, connect, and grow. Recognizing that skilled crafters often struggle with visibility and monetization, I led end-to-end product discovery to define what a creator-first marketplace could look like — from user pain points to core feature prioritization.

  • Conducted user interviews with 15+ independent creators to uncover core friction points around discovery and pricing
  • Synthesized research into a prioritized feature set using the RICE framework, balancing reach, impact, and effort
  • Defined the MVP scope and built a user story map to align stakeholders on phased delivery
  • Designed low-fidelity wireframes in Figma to validate the core user journey before any development began
